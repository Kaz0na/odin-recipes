Spicy Fish

An incredibly easy dish to make for which you need no more than five ingredients. But as the name implies this dish is not for the faint of heart so if you're not a fan of anything spicy I would advise steering clear of it. And if you're diabetic you should probably keep some insulin handy because there is a lot of sugar involved.

Ingredients

  • 2 Panga Fillets
  • A bunch of brown sugar
  • A bunch of extra spicy sambal badjak
  • Sesame seeds
  • Nut rice

Preparation

  1. If frozen defrost the fillets
  2. Grease up an oven dish with some butter and preheat the oven to 180º C.
  3. Put on a pot of water and wait for it to boil. Once boiling add the nut rice and let cook for 8 minutes.
  4. While the rice is cooking put the fish in the oven dish. Coat the fish with a good helping of sambal and then a good second layer of brown sugar, topped off with a good sprinkling of sesame seeds.
  5. Drain the cooked rice and let sit for about 10 minutes.
  6. In the meantime pop the fish dish into the oven and set a timer for 10-12 minutes.
  7. Once the fish is done take it from the oven and plate it together with the rice.
