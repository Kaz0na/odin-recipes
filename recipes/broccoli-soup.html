Broccoli Soup with Cheese

Description

Creamy broccoli soup with some old cheese for extra flavour. The combination of onion, broccoli, potatoes and vegetable stock make this a good hardy soup. Finished with some cooking dairy to make it extra rich and creamy. Serve with a piece of toasted bread and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 25g Butter
  • 2 Onions
  • 1l Vegetable Stock
  • 2 Floury Potatoes
  • 250ml Cooking Dairy
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 50g Grated Old Cheese

Utensils

  • Blender
  • Grater
  • Fine Sieve

Preparation

  1. Melt the butter in a pan on low to medium heat. Saute the onions for 4 minutes without letting them discolor. Add the broccoli and let it bake gently for 5 minutes.
  2. Add the vegetable stock and the potatoes and bring up to a boil and let it boil for 10 minutes.
  3. Take the pan with the soup off the heat and puree ith with the blender. Add the cooking dairy and reheat for about 2 minutes. Add salt and pepper for flavour.
  4. Pour the soup through the sieve into a second pan. Then divide the soup over bowls and sprinkle some old cheese on top.
